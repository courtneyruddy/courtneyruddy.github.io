BIOGRAPHY

I am a University of Maryland, College Park senior, majoring in multi-platform journalism with a concentration in film. I began my journalism career with a mentorship program with Kare 11 News in Minneapolis. From there I started to direct and produce commercials for local businesses. In college I have been a staff writer for our campus women's magazine, as well as managed the social media accounts for different organizations on campus.

Writer, photographer, videographer, and social media manager.
